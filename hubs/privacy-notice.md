# {octicon}`shield-lock` OGGM Hubs Privacy Statement

_Last updated: 09.03.2026_

Welcome to the privacy statement for **OGGM Hub** and **OGGM Classroom**. This document explains how personal data is processed when you use the OGGM Hubs.

"Personal data" means any information relating to an identified or identifiable person.

---

## Scope

This Privacy Statement applies to the services:

- **hub.oggm.org**
- **classroom.oggm.org**

These services are operated by **OGGM e.V.**, which acts as the **Data Controller** for personal data processed through the OGGM Hubs.

Technical infrastructure for the hubs is hosted on servers operated by the **University of Bremen**, which provides infrastructure and system administration.

For privacy information related to other OGGM websites and services, please refer to the general OGGM privacy notice: [](../legal).

---

## Intended use

OGGM Hub and OGGM Classroom are provided for **research and teaching purposes related to OGGM**.

The hubs are shared computing environments and are not intended for the storage or processing of sensitive or confidential data.

OGGM e.V. reserves the right to suspend or terminate access in cases of misuse or activities that threaten the stability, security, or intended purpose of the service.

---

## Personal data processed

When using the OGGM Hubs, the following types of personal data may be processed.

### Account information

- username
- encrypted password

### Technical logs

System logs generated by the hub infrastructure may contain:

- IP address
- timestamps of user actions
- authentication events
- system activity related to the operation of the service

These logs are used solely for the operation, maintenance, and security of the service.

---

## Legal basis for processing

Personal data is processed on the basis of **legitimate interest (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR)**, specifically for:

- providing access to the computing service
- maintaining system security
- detecting misuse or technical problems
- operating and improving the infrastructure

---

## Data storage and retention

User account information is stored for as long as an account remains active.

Users may request deletion of their account at any time by contacting:

**info@oggm.org**

System logs that may contain IP addresses are retained for **up to 90 days** and are automatically deleted thereafter.

User files and data stored within the computing environment remain under the responsibility of the user.

---

## Administrative access

Administrators of the OGGM Hubs may access system information necessary for operating and maintaining the service. This may include:

- viewing which accounts are active
- reviewing system logs
- accessing user storage when required for technical maintenance or security investigations

Administrative access is limited to operational purposes and is not used for routine monitoring of user activity.

---

## Analytics

The OGGM Hubs use **Plausible Analytics**, a privacy-friendly analytics service.

Plausible Analytics:

- does **not use cookies**
- does **not track users across websites**
- does **not collect personally identifiable information**

It provides aggregated statistics that help improve documentation and services.

More information:
https://plausible.io/data-policy

---

## Cookies

The OGGM Hubs do **not use tracking or advertising cookies**.

Some technical components of the authentication system may use **temporary session cookies** that are strictly necessary for the operation of the service.

These cookies are required for login and security functionality.

---

## Responsibility for data and backups

The OGGM Hubs are provided free of charge as a service to the community.

While reasonable efforts are made to maintain the infrastructure, **OGGM e.V. cannot guarantee data persistence or service availability**.

Users are responsible for maintaining backups of their own work and should not store sensitive or irreplaceable data on the hubs.

---

## Your rights

Under applicable data protection laws, including the **General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)**, users have rights regarding their personal data, including the right to:

- request access to personal data
- request correction or deletion
- object to certain types of processing

Requests may be sent to:

**info@oggm.org**

---

## Changes to this statement

This privacy statement may be updated periodically to reflect changes in the operation of the OGGM Hubs or applicable regulations.

The most recent version will always be available on this page.
